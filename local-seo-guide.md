# Local SEO Guide

## What is Local SEO?

Local SEO helps businesses appear in Google Search and Google Maps for nearby customers.

## Local SEO Checklist

- Create Google Business Profile
- Add Business Name, Address, and Phone
- Collect Customer Reviews
- Use Local Keywords
- Create Location Pages
- Optimize Website Speed
- Add Schema Markup

## Benefits

- More Local Customers
- Higher Google Maps Rankings
- Increased Phone Calls
- Better Brand Visibility
