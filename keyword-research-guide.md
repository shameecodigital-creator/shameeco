# Keyword Research Guide

## What is Keyword Research?

Keyword research is the process of finding the words and phrases that people search for on Google. It helps businesses create content that matches user intent and improves search engine rankings.

## Types of Keywords

- Short-tail Keywords
- Long-tail Keywords
- Local Keywords
- Informational Keywords
- Transactional Keywords

## Best Free Keyword Research Tools

- Google Keyword Planner
- Google Trends
- Google Search Suggestions
- AnswerThePublic
- Ubersuggest (Free Version)

## Tips

- Focus on user intent.
- Target low-competition keywords.
- Use keywords naturally.
- Include keywords in titles and headings.
